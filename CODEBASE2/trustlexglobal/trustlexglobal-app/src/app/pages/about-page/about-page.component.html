Our Story

TRUSTLEX Global was born from a simple frustration: navigating international business formation shouldn't require a law degree, a consultant's budget, and months of trial and error.

Our founding team has built businesses across the US, UAE, and Asia. We've sat on your side of the table — dealing with confusing regulations, slow government processes, and service providers who disappeared after taking payment.

That experience shaped our approach: one dedicated advisor per client, proactive communication, and a genuine investment in your success. We don't just file paperwork — we become the partner you can call when the stakes are high and the answers aren't obvious.
